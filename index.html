Greece is the place where the Acropolis of Athens stands tall, like a high city on a rocky hill. It's an ancient place full of amazing old buildings and art. These old buildings are the best examples of Greek history and culture. The most famous one is called The Parthenon, and it's super cool!

Long ago, this volcano made Santorini what it is today. But Santorini isn't just about the volcano. It's also famous for its beautiful white buildings with blue roofs. They're everywhere you look, and they're so pretty!
The Olympics Did you know that Greece had the first ever Olympic Games? Yep, way back in 776 BC, people from all over Greece gathered to run, jump, wrestle, and more.
